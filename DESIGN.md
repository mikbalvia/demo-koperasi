## Style Prompt

Cinematic civic cooperative: an official red-white Indonesian identity reframed like a documentary title sequence. The interface should feel grounded, trustworthy, and atmospheric: deep warm charcoal, heritage red, quiet gold, textured light, large editorial typography, and slow reveal motion. Visual depth comes from real imagery, film grain, hairline rules, masked crops, and high-contrast surfaces rather than decorative blobs.

## Colors

- `#120f0d` charcoal stage: primary dark canvas and footer base
- `#4a0d0d` deep heritage red: hero wash and dramatic accents
- `#b32025` civic red: primary action and active navigation
- `#d8a84e` muted gold: official highlights, dividers, and metadata
- `#f7f2e8` warm paper: light section background and readable surfaces
- `#0f4a3c` cooperative green: secondary trust signal used sparingly

## Typography

- Display: `Newsreader`, Georgia, serif. Use for large cinematic headings and brand moments.
- Body: `IBM Plex Sans`, system-ui, sans-serif. Use for navigation, paragraphs, labels, and UI.
- Data: inherit body with `font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums`.

## Motion

- Entrances start after a short beat, then reveal in staggered layers: metadata first, headline second, actions and panels last.
- Prefer slow push-ins, mask wipes, and opacity with subtle translate/scale. Avoid playful bounce.
- Ambient motion should be restrained: image drift, light sweep, and grain only.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use gradient text, decorative orbs, neon cyan/purple, or generic glass blobs.
- Do not center every section into a symmetrical stack; anchor content to clear left/right zones.
- Do not over-round cards; keep framed surfaces sharp, civic, and editorial.
- Do not make the palette all red. Use paper, charcoal, gold, and restrained green to balance it.
